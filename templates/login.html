What is MyHSC?

MyHSC is a dedicated study companion designed specifically for HSC students in New South Wales. It provides tools and features tailored to the Stage 6 Syllabus, including syllabus- linked tracking, study blogs, stopwatch, timer, flashcards. MyHSC aims to help you stay organised, motivated, and on track throughout your HSC journey—all in a distraction-free, ad-free environment.
