Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/08/2025

App Name: MyHSC Team

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our HSC study app. By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We are committed to collecting only the information that is necessary for the proper functioning of the app and your user experience. We do not collect more data than required, and we never sell your personal information.

a. Personal Information

When you create an account and use our app, we may collect the following information:

  • Email Address: used for account login, communication, and password recovery
  • Chosen password: used for account security and authentication, encrypted and securely stored
  • Selected HSC subjects: for suitable content and resources

This information is collected when you:

  • Register for an account or log in to the app
  • Select or update your study subjects
  • Use features that rely on subject-based organisation

We do NOT collect fullnames, phone numbers, home addresses, payment information or sensitive personal identifiers.

2. How we use your infomation

We use your information solely to support and enhance your experience within the app.

Specifically, we use the data to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Personalise your dashboard with subject-specific tools
  • Track your study progress and preferences
  • Provide reminder notifications based on your study plans
  • Respond to any technical support requests you submit

We do not use your data for targeted advertising, profiling, or analytics unrelated to your study experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or alteration.

  • All passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text.
  • Our servers use secure encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) to protect your data in transit.
  • Access to stored data is strictly limited to essential system processes and authorised administrators.
  • We regularly review our security practices to ensure your information is safeguarded.

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities as required by applicable laws.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We value your trust and handle your data with care. We do not:

  • Sell your data to advertisers or third parties.
  • Share your data with external services for marketing or promotional purposes.

We many share data only under the following conditions:

  • When required by law or a valid legal request
  • If necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public

In all other cases, your data remains strictly private.

5. User Rights and Control

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can manage your account settings within the app to:

  • Update your email address or password
  • Change your selected HSC subjects
  • Delete your account and all associated data

When tou delete your account:

  • All your personal information will be permanently removed from our servers.
  • If you have any questions or concerns about your data, please contact us at Your study progress and preferences will be irretrievable.

If you need help managing your account, please contact us at: myhsc@gmail.com

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for students generally aged 16 and older who are enrolled in the NSW Higher School Certificate (HSC) program. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Post the revised version in the app and on our website
  • Update the “Effective Date” at the top of this policy
  • Notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification

We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed of how we are protecting your data.
